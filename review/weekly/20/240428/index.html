中文版的周报

组织新的一周的周报：

本周开始专注于Victima模拟器的数据读取逻辑拓展，理解了部分的访存逻辑，并且已经修正了4个空指针/野指针/数据越界导致的segment fault的问题。现在发现了Victima模拟内存访问时，使用多线程模拟了内存访问请求在类似TCP的网络中传输的过程。这部分的seg fault由于涉及多线程，比较复杂，还有待进一步的研究。下周将进一步研究，以尝试进一步理解其实现的细节和设计的初衷。

Dear Qingcai,

Here is a summary of this week’s achievements and my planned activities for the upcoming week.

This Week’s Progress:

  • Expansion of Data Retrieval Logic in Victima Simulator

    • I have focused on extending the data retrieval logic of the Victima Simulator. I gained an understanding of some of its memory access logic and successfully resolved four issues related to null pointers, dangling pointers, and out-of-bounds data accesses, which were causing segmentation faults.

  • Multi-threaded Memory Access Simulation:

    • I encountered a complex scenario where the Victima Simulator simulates memory access requests transmitted over a network similar to TCP using multi-threading. This aspect is intricate due to its multi-threaded nature and is currently under further investigation due to persistent segmentation faults.

Goals for Next Week:

  • In-depth Study of Multi-threaded Issues:
    • The upcoming week will be dedicated to further studying the multi-threaded aspects of the memory access simulation. My goal is to delve deeper into the implementation details and understand the design motivations behind these features, particularly to resolve the segmentation faults and enhance the simulator’s stability.
